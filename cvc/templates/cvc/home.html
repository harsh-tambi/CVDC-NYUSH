About Us

Collective Voice is NYU Shanghai’s leading English-language debate club focused on multiple styles of debating: British Parliamentary, American Parliamentary, Chinese, etc. We train NYU Shanghai students in these informal and formal debating styles to prepare them for intervarsity debate tournaments within China and internationally.

One of the most academically demanding and rewarding experiences that NYU Shanghai can provide is debating. Collective Voice Debate Club assists individuals in acquiring argumentation skills, good judgment, self-assurance, and the ability to act quickly under pressure. Debating diverse issues involving international public policy, current affairs, sciences, ethics, and philosophy. We equip our members with in-depth research, keen observational skills, and an intellectual foundation. Assisted debates with a mentor's guidance promote flexibility and productive interaction. All of these abilities remain with us for the rest of our lives and are crucial to both our personal and professional success.

We regulalry hold:

  • Intensive mentorship sessions in American Parliamentary, British Parliamentary, and Chinese Debate.
  • Monthly Competitive Debating Tournaments.
  • Round Table Intimate Discussions About Politics, Social Sciences, STEM, Educatipn, etc.
